About Sen Spa


Attached to the beauty of a simple, sincere, romantic flower but with a strong will to live, Sen Spa is designed and decorated with a rustic, simple style imbued with traditional Vietnamese characteristics.

Located in the center of Ho Chi Minh City, Sen Spa is separated from the noisy and bustling atmosphere, opening a quiet and gentle space. Customers coming to Sen Spa will be able to step into a luxurious space, alternating between modern and classic, hiding the image of elegant and gentle lotus flowers filled with the layout here.

With about 30 technicians recruited and 100% trained by Sen Spa, the quality of workmanship is according to international standards, so it can satisfy all discerning and fastidious guests at home and abroad.

History begin


2004


An Nam Spa was born at 26-28 Dong Du, District 1 is an independent building with 9 floors dedicated to serving guests.

2006


Sen Spa was opened at 21 Nguyen Trung Ngan, District 1 with 3 floors (9-10-11) in Miss Aodai building.

2014


Merged An Nam Spa & Sen Spa at location 26-28 Dong Du, District 1 into SEN SPA.

2015


Open more SEN VIET SPA including 2 points at the international departure terminal and 1 point at the domestic departure terminal at Tan Son Nhat airport.

2019


Moving Sen Spa location to 10B1 Le Thanh Ton, District 1 is an independent building with 6 spa floors and 1 serviced apartment for rent.
